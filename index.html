Short Biography

I am a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Peter Wonka at the Visual Computing Center, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology ( KAUST). I received my Bachelor degree in computational mathematics from Wuhan University. Since 2009, I was at the Department of Mathematics, Zhejiang University, where I received my Ph.D. in 2014. My advisor was Prof. Ligang Liu. Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

My research interests are in the area of computer graphics and computer vision (shape analysis, interactive modeling, procedural modeling, ...).
